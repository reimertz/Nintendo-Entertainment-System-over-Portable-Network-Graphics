Play

Screen

If screen changes without your interaction, don't be afraid! It means that someone else in the world is playing together with you. If you are curious how many you play together with, just look in the top-left corner.

Controller

Press the any of the buttons on the controller to interact with the game, just like you would do if you had the controller in your hand.
  • ARROWS - move the character
  • SELECT - pause/unpause the game, menu selection
  • START - menu
  • A/B - action buttons
